# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful and considerate.
- Assume good intent and focus on constructive feedback.
- No harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.

## Enforcement
- Project maintainers may take appropriate action for any unacceptable behavior, including warnings or bans.

## Reporting
- Report unacceptable behavior to the maintainers via issues or other contact channels listed in the repository.
